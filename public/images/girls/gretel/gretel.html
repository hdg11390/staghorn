Staghorn's Mirror Image (call name: Gretel)

(Ch Staghorn's Hide And Go Zeek X Ch Staghorn's Reflections CGN)


D.O.B. July 18, 2016

 

Cerf exam clear, Hips, elbows and genetic testing pending

 

Proudly bred from two Champion Staghorn dogs , Gretel is a dream. We love her silly and yet gentle sense of humour and her dense, double coat. After a slow to mature start (often the best kind) she's coming nicely into her own now and will be making her debut in the show ring this year. A wonderful girl, she's very easy to live with and keeps us smiling. We expect great things with this fun gal. With her parents excellent clearances, looks and brains, we have nothing but the highest of hopes for our girlie.
