Staghorn's Sillouette (call name: Shade)

(Ch. Staghorn's Jimmy Hollywood X GCH. Staghorn's Reflection in Black)

 

Our Miss Shade, proudly co-bred with my dear friend Chantelle Bennett of Benacres Labradors, is a lovely addition to our line up. Best Baby Puppy in Specialty Shows at both the LOC and the Quebec specialties in 2016 and also champion pointed, she has matured into a lovely bitch with a great head and coat, good angels and ample leg under her. I'm very pleased with this girl.


D.O.B. January 17, 2016


PRA and EIC Clear by Parentage

OFA Hips and Elbows pending, Cerf Clear
