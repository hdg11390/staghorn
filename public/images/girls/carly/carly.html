Ch. Staghorn's Kindest Spirit (call name: Carly)

(Am GCh Epochs Mocassin Joe X GCh Staghorn 'N Kindspirit Have It)


D.O.B. May 24, 2014

 

OFA Excellent Hips and Elbows Normal, Cerf Clear,
PRA, EIC, CNM, HNPK, RD/OD1, SD2, DM clear

 

Proudly co-bred with my dear friend Carla Vatour of Kindspirit Labradors in NB, Carly is a dream. We love her silly and yet gentle sense of humour and her dark, beautiful coat. After a slow to mature start (often the best kind) she's coming nicely into her own now and will be making her debut in the show ring this fall/winter. A wonderful house dog, she's very easy to live with and keeps us smiling. We expect great things with this fun gal. With her string of excellent clearances, her good looks and brains, we have the highest of hopes for our girlie.
