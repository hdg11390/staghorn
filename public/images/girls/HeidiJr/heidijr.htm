Staghorn's Hidin' At Skyland (a.k.a. Heidi)

(Ch. Applemeadow Marshall Mac X Skyland's Hidin' at Staghorn)


DOB. November 14, 2006


Hips/Elbows Clear, CERF ACVO cleared annually

Heart: "Normal" - Cardiologist - ECHO/Doppler

 

Our Heidi Jr. headed off as a baby to Nova Scotia, to the home/kennel her mother, our Heidi Sr. was born at (Skyland). Skyland decided to no longer breed Labradors, and kindly returned Heidi Jr. to us. I'm still aching all over from the cartwheels I did at the airport when I saw how lovely she matured. She is a rich dark chocolate, nice brown eyes, beautiful head, coat and bone. Her temperament is fabulous. Heidi Jr. has all her clearances and we are thrilled to have her join the ranks of the Staghorn Girls.
