Castlegar Staghorn Stacy(call name: Stacy)

(CH Oaksill Castlegar Vixen X Am GCH Caer Bren McLeod, JH)


D.O.B. November 24, 2014

 

OFA Hips and Elbows pending, EIC tested Clear

 

While slow to mature, our sweet Miss Stacy is well worth waiting for. A huge call out of thanks to Pierry and Stan McLean for allowing this darling girl to come to Staghorn. We expect that after a maturity period, this girl will be a definite asset to our program. Her pedigree is impeccable. Meantime, she's a definite asset to our home, loving the couch life and already marking her own lazy girl chair to curl up in. She's extremely loving and is exactly what folks want time and again, in a Labrador female.
