He now also shares the home with his little yellow buddy, Dustin, our Snoopy son. Both boys have an EXCELLENT home with this family and we truly enjoy our friendship with them. You've not seen two luckier or more well cared for dogs than these two lucky boys. Louis is available at stud to approved females.
