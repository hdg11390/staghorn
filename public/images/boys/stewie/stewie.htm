Am CH/ BISS Can GCH. Staghorn's Harris Tweed (a.k.a Stewie)

(Am/Can Ch Staghorn's Bearridge Caliber X Can Ch Castlegar Lubberline The Eagle)

Major pointed in the USA (including a Specialty win)


D.O.B. October 26, 2010


OFA Hips Good, Elbows Normal, Optigen Clear, Annual CERF
Cardiac ECHO clear, EIC carrier by parentage


Stewie is my pride and joy. He recently won Best in Specialty and Best Bred By Exhibitor in Specialty, under respected Labrador Breeder Judge, Sally Bell of Borador's Reg'd (USA). Then little more than a month later, and despite being out of coat, an Award of Merit under respected Judge Leslie Grant, of Gorsebrook Reg'd. We couldn't be prouder of this handsome showman. A pleasure to live with and a joy to show, I am always so proud to walk into any ring with my home bred lovely, compact black boy. Everyone who knows Stewie at the shows knows he's quite the comedian and loves to insist on all your hugs and kisses. He is well muscled, short coupled, with excellent front and rear angles and lots of thigh and second thigh, just the way we love them. Sire to champion and therapy dog get, Mr. Stew will wow you with his all round quality and oomph.
