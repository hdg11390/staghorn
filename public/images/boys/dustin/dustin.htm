Staghorn's Nothing But Destiny (call name: Dustin)
(Am Can CH. Sunspot's Nothing But Net x Staghorn's Destiny's Child)

Date of Birth: November 05, 2008
OFA Fair Hips (taken at 7 years old), Elbows Normal, Cerf Clear,
EIC Clear, Optigen Clear

This is our handsome Snoopy son, we're very proud of him. Thank you Snoopy and Dusty for such a beautiful boy. Dustin is so much like Snoopy. Several of Snoopy's American friends and fans have inquired about him. Dustin's dam Dusty) is a daughter of Am Can Ch Boradors Exch at Devonsleigh, who was actually a littermate to Am Ch Borador By George (Gordy). Dusty's mother was from our old, proven sound Lindenhall/Staghorn dogs. Dustin is a solid boy with lots of leg under him, good turn of stifle and orthopedic soundness, really nice front angles to match the rear and nice forechest, good genetic clearances and most wonderful of all, he has his sire's wonderful temperament. Dustin is available at stud to approved females. With his pedigree and clearances and his own excellent health and soundness, we believe he has a lot to offer.
