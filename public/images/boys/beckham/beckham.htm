Ch. Staghorn's Blame it on Beckham (a.k.a. Beckham)

Date of Birth: Jan 11, 2008
OVC Hips/Elbows Clear, Optigen non affected carrier, EIC Clear,

Heart: "Normal" - Cardiologist - ECHO/Doppler

Our handsome champion Beckham has been turning heads in both Canada and the USA. He is of good size, length and height. His lovely dark chocolate coat is correct in colour and texture. He has one of those big, beautiful heads without being overdone. He has been in the ribbons at US specialties under such expert judges as Jean Louis Blais. We have received a great deal of positive feedback and interest in this boy. Like his chocolate half siblings here, his lovely otter tail never stops wagging thanks to their happy and outgoing personalities. He's a very calm and smart boy, very easy to manage and to live with. We proudly co-own him with Julie Ellis of Breezeline Labradors. Julie and I couldn't be happier with Beckham.
